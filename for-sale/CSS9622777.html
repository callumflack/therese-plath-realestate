Residential property at EARLVILLE on a 771m2

Mid $300's Range

49 Yarrum Street, EARLVILLE 3 1 2

Land area: 771m2

Property ID: CSS9622777

Therese Plath presents this immaculately presented solid brick family home in excellent condition and is ready and waiting for you to move straight into. Offering character and charm and perfectly positioned on a generous, level 771m2 block in an enviable, quiet street within walking distance to public transport, local shops, OLHOC Primary School and Earlville Shopping Centre and a short drive to Cairns City Centre and famous Esplanade.

This well presented home presents three generous bedrooms with built-in wardrobes and easy access to the spacious bathroom. The lounge area is positioned at the front of the home with adjoining dining room and kitchen which flows on to the secure, undercover outdoor living. The property enjoys a picturesque mountainous backdrop with plenty of room for children and pets to play in the safe fenced backyard. For the discerning chef, the kitchen space features electric cooktop and oven, easy-to-access drawers and dishwasher.

The delightful low maintenance gardens surrounding the home provide a sanctuary for the garden enthusiast. Additional features include tandem garage, security screens, ceiling fans, air-conditioning, linen press, large laundry area and garden shed.

This property is well priced for a quick sale at the asking price of mid $300's range. Therese Plath invites you to visit this superb home or call me on 0418 772 995 or email on therese.plath@raywhite.com to arrange your inspection today!
