Residential property at BENTLEY PARK on a 660m2

$520,000

60 Fitzmaurice Drive, BENTLEY PARK 5 3 2

Land area: 660m2

Property ID: CSS9539413

Therese Plath says the living is easy in this private impeccably presented family residence. Set in a family friendly area this quality home is an ideal retreat for busy families with spacious living areas merging seamlessly through outside entertaining.

Slighted elevated and perfectly located in the popular sought-after area of Silkwood Ridge, Cairns which is nearby to a number of private, state & Catholic schools, walking paths, parks, and so close to local shops including the Edmonton shopping centre and the popular Sugar World Water Park only 3 minutes away and a short 20 minute drive to Cairns CBD and the International & Domestic Airport.

This very generous home presents a striking minimalist creation that offers life and style in equal measure an artful blend of razor straight edges, expressive details and an innovative floor plan that creates an intimate relationship between every room.

The well-equipped modern gourmet style kitchen overlooks the adjoining dining area and features stylish stone bench tops and sleek cabinetry with extensive storage, with walk in pantry ensuring optimal functionality with premium stainless steel appliances, including gas cooktop and electric oven which services the comfortable living area.

The heart of this most stunning residence is the modern kitchen which merges the lounge and dining areas producing an open and welcoming space with sliding doors opening onto the private entertaining patio, which blur the distinction between indoor and outdoor living. This fusion of light and openness is complemented throughout the home.

The parents retreat offers expansive space with en-suite and walk in robe and an inviting sitting area with the other 3 bedrooms enjoying easy access to the generous family sized bathroom with the bonus of the 5th bedroom having its own en-suite.

Offering two separate living areas with the option of working from home in the massive media room with an adjoining office. The children's bedrooms are all large with build in cupboards.

The home is fully air-conditioned and security screened with abundance of storage plus double remote car accommodation and the back garden is fully fenced providing a safe haven for children and pets.

Imagine lazy afternoon relaxing around the swimming pool enjoying the lovely views to the surrounding mountain backdrop.

A quality home in a fabulous, quiet location - be quick to avoid disappointment. Therese Plath 0418 772 995 or email therese.plath@raywhite.com
