Therese Plath is excited to present to the market this perfectly perched elevated family home in Upper Mount Sheridan, which captures all the summer breezes and boasts magical views.

Surrender yourself to a true representation of family living maximising its elevated position, capturing fabulous views and offers a secure and private existence. An abundance of natural light and fresh breezes compliments this modern home which has quality fixtures and fittings throughout.

First class finishes, and neutral tones, complement the exceptionally well planned floor layout to create a comfortable abode. Built for the entertainer and perfect for your family, take advantage of the air-conditioned open plan living areas.

The main living and dining area incorporates the fabulous and impressive gourmet entertainers' kitchen with corner pantry superbly appointed with first class stainless steel appliances with wide fridge space, stone bench tops, glass splash back and banks of cupboard and storage spaces and effortlessly opens out to a delightful elevated alfresco area with direct access to the tiled entertaining patio and sparkling pool.

All four bedrooms are spacious, carpeted and air-conditioned with the master bedroom having their very own 5m2 walk in robe and large en-suite with double shower and main bathroom with spa.

A catalogue of features include:

  • Garden shed to rear

Meticulously presented and truly one of the best positions in Mount Sheridan, just 10 minutes from Cairns city centre.
