Residential property at EDMONTON on a 732m2

Low $400's Range

18 Arkendeith Drive, EDMONTON 4 2 2

Land area: 732m2

Property ID: CTR8575306

Therese Plath is excited to present this quality home which offers the ultimate in family living with a free-flowing, light filled floor plan which will instantly impress.

Perfectly positioned opposite family friendly open parkland and set on a securely tucked away 732m2 block in a quiet nook, this true family-sized, fully air conditioned home provides every amenity you need in a very sensible and workable design.

Only three minutes to a Primary School and just 5 minutes drive to choice of schools, shopping centres and even popular Sugar World Water Park and an easy 15 minute drive if you work in the City.

As you enter the property you will be impressed with the immaculate established gardens and on walking through the entrance the space inside is immediately noticeable. The massive open plan design flows through to the kitchen, dining and lounge area with separate media room.

The central kitchen has electric stainless steel appliances, ample bench and cupboard space, corner pantry and room for double fridge.

The master bedroom is complete with en-suite and walk-in wardrobe and each of the other bedrooms are a good size with built in cupboards. The main family bathroom has shower, bath and single vanity.

The good living continues outside with the tiled covered entertaining area which overlooks the safe, fenced backyard. Manicured lawns and wide side access to the rear, plus there is ample room to erect a massive shed and a swimming pool.

All this set among other quality homes with guaranteed quality neighbours makes your lifestyle perfect.

One viewing of this spacious, family home you'll be hooked! We look forward to showing you. Please contact Therese Plath for an inspection on 0418 772 995 or email Therese at therese.plath@raywhite.com
