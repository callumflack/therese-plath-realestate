Residential property at EDMONTON on a 8053m2

Mid $600's Range

179-181 Wiseman Road West, EDMONTON 5 2 2

Land area: 8053m2

Property ID: CSS9540836

Therese Plath is excited to offer this immaculately presented home so conveniently located and only 15 minutes from Cairns City Centre. Nestled amongst a myriad of acreage properties, this property avails any prospective buyer with a laidback lifestyle seldom found this close to suburbia. With a natural vista and broad frontage, discerning buyers will be captivated by the overall blissful layout and generous proportions which provide this leafy family retreat. This quality family retreat set upon 8053m2 of flat and gently sloping land and possible future potential to sub-divide if desired.

Located within a short drive to all amenities, including state and private schools, sports precincts, shopping centres and transport hubs, but positioned far enough away to enjoy the country lifestyle that we all aspire too. The house has been setback from the road so that your privacy is assured this seller regrettably is relocating and sadly leaving behind lots of happy times and memories.

With views of the tranquil surrounds and the mountain backdrop, the outdoor space is simply enviable! Plenty of space to kick back and watch the kids enjoy the extensive grounds complete with chicken coop and is fully fenced.

Open and spacious, living and dining are strategically positioned with ease of access to the private outdoor entertaining patio accessed through double sliding glass doors seamlessly bringing the outdoors in and allowing a wealth of natural light and breezes to flow through and has neutral tones throughout accentuated by tiled flooring creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.

A testament to quality, the impressive and delightful modern kitchen allows for easy service to the dining are designed with and the finest of fittings ensuring the perfect sanctuary for aspiring chefs with double oven with gas cooktop, dishwasher and plenty of bench and cupboard spaces complete with walk in pantry - cooking here will be a delight.

The master suite is an oversized haven with spacious en-suite, double vanities, large shower, plunge bath and separate toilet and sizable walk-in robe plus four other generous bedrooms.

Proudly perched in an established popular area of Edmonton with the seasonal creek running through the property - Here lies the perfect opportunity to own a piece of paradise where you can have a horse or pony to roam free in your backyard.

Positioned to ensure peace and tranquility yet maintain the convenience of being close to all amenities, this acreage offers the ideal family lifestyle quietly tucked away and waiting for you!! Phone for your inspection with Therese Plath on 0418 772995 or email on therese.plath@raywhite.com
