Residential property at LITTLE MULGRAVE on a 10.6m2

Low to Mid $700's Range

, LITTLE MULGRAVE 4 2 2

Land area: 10.6m2

Property ID: CSS9986064

Therese Plath is excited to present this hidden treasure. This spectacular property is positioned high and proud commanding views of the mountains which dance around it.

The home indulges in an exceptionally private position with two acres of usable land and the other surrounded by rainforest and creek setting. A rare and impressive property purchase opportunity, all buyers are invited to experience the refreshing breezes and blissful bird song.

Follow the concreted driveway to this classic Colonial designed home which will endure the passing of time, providing the ideal environment for your family to grow with wrap around timber verandas which captures cooling breezes whilst offering a seamless flow from indoor to outdoor living whilst looking over the resort style swimming pool.

Whilst maintaining its character the home offers a family-centred design, focused on comfort with a relaxed yet sophisticated, lodge feel created by open living and entertaining spaces that effortlessly intertwine with polished timber floors and high ceilings throughout.

The kitchen merges with the living and dining areas, providing a central point for gathering and entertaining with silky oak timber benches, 900cm gas cook top and oven, dishwasher and plenty of cupboard and bench space for every chef in the house to enjoy.

This flawless expression of fit and finish is completed by wide polished timber floors, a breakfast bar and a range of quality stainless steel appliances.

There are four bedrooms in total with a massive master suite, large en suite with dual basins, walk in robe and glass doors opening onto the veranda taking in views. The other three bedrooms have easy access to the main bathroom spa bath, shower, single vanity and separate toilet.

For those who love horses we have found a safe horse haven with a 3 bay stable, complete with water, fans, power, feed shed, dressage arena and fenced horse paddock for approximately 3 horses.

Other features are:

  • Small office/storeroom or could be craft room
  • Enclosed sun room to front of house
  • Fully landscaped and easy care gardens
  • Excellent storage throughout
  • Internal laundry
  • Gas hot water
  • 2 bay shed with lean to with room extend if required
  • Local school bus at the front of the property

Spanning across a single level this property is a safe haven for families who enjoy privacy and peace with a seasonal creek on the boundary the perfect playground. Call Therese 0418 772 995 therese.plath@raywhite.com
