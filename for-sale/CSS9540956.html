Therese Plath is excited to present this fabulous near new master piece with outstanding pool side entertaining with only neighbours on one side with an abundance of natural light, glass and elegant style.

The spacious contemporary style home of superior design is awaiting your inspection. Once you walk in the impressive front entrance you will be thrilled at to what is on offer. Punctuated by modern finishes with extra high ceilings, porcelain tiles and neutral tones and an abundance of natural light compliment this very stylish home.

The spacious 308m2 floor plan balances distinct rooms with open and flowing living areas with separate media room and open plan dining, lounge and kitchen which glass sliding doors that extend out to the alfresco living area which creates a seamless link between indoors and out ensuring an ideal layout for entertaining with built in outdoor kitchen, complete with barbeque, amble cupboard and bench spaces and exhaust.

The gourmet kitchen showcases first class cabinetry with stone benches and a full range of stainless steel appliances with slow closing drawers, double fridge space, gas cook top, warming oven, electric oven and built in microwave. The chef of the home will be impressed with the enormous amount of bench and cupboard spaces

There are many other standout features that go with home and the list is never ending.

Manicured, low maintenance gardens with wide side access for your boat, caravan, camper or trailer.

Therese is looking forward to showing you this exclusive, fabulous home situated in a very private, gated community with a step and a jump to the Paradise Palms Golf Course with direct path access to 6th hole) and no rear and only one side neighbours and beaches only moments away.
