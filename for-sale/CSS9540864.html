Residential property at EDMONTON on a 800m2

Offers in the Low $500,000s

11 Gates Close, EDMONTON 4 2 2

Land area: 800m2

Property ID: CSS9540864

Therese Plath is delighted to introduce this beautifully presented family home to the market. Perfectly located backing onto friendly open parklands so close to shopping and easy access to local schools and only a short 20 minute drive to Cairns City Centre.

Offering peace and tranquillity you will be thrilled at the content on offer along with a style and feel that just beckons for you to call home sweet home.

The generous floor plan emphasises a sophisticated design - an entertainer's haven revealing a large tiled living space with glass sliders bringing the outside in, allowing abundant natural light and breezes to flow throughout. This dual indoor/outdoor living and entertaining area is impressive, so spacious and is complete with outdoor kitchen overlooking the privately fenced back yard to the picturesque mountain backdrop.

Gourmet and designed specifically for the chef the well-equipped modern kitchen overlooks the adjoining dining area featuring stylish stone bench tops and sleek cabinetry with extensive storage, separate butler's pantry ensuring optimal functionality with premium appliances, including gas cooktop and electric oven which services the comfortable living area. For the movie buff the separate media room has ample space for lazy days or could be the children's playground.

The separate master suite features generous en-suite with spa bath, double vanities, quality stone bench tops, double shower large walk in robe with direct access through sliding doors to rear yard. There are three other spacious bedrooms, all built in and having direct access to the family bathroom with plunge bath, shower and vanity.

The internal laundry is spacious and has ample bench, folding and cupboard space. The generous double remote car accommodation has direct internal access through the laundry or kitchen.

For the tradesman there is a 15 x 4m powered shed with easy rear access what a dream!

Proudly constructed by Cougar Homes using the finest building materials, the timeless architecture ensures a stately street presence that will impress the most fastidious purchaser.
