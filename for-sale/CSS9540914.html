Residential property at EDMONTON on a 5498m2

High $600's Range

In a class of its own Therese Plath is excited to offer this piece of paradise set on a private, secure 5,498m2 parcel of land and only 20 minutes to Cairns city centre and famous Esplanade, with Edmonton shopping centre, schools and every convenience just 5 minutes away - ideal for a growing family with room to live and play surrounded by nature.

This expansive, solidly built, wonderfully presented double brick family home offers 5 bedrooms with master suite having extra-large walk in robe and semi en-suite with three other bedrooms on the top level.

The galley kitchen is a delight to cook in and is perfectly positioned for easy access to living, dining and outdoor entertaining creating a seamless connection between indoors and out. Indulge yourself in your little world of tranquillity & privacy whilst entertaining on the massive undercover tiled entertaining patio which overlooks the fenced manicured rear yard.

On the lower level four car accommodation / 12.2m x 7m work shop, office and fifth bedroom offering abundant additional space plus second toilet and shower and internal laundry with shute. Both levels have high ceilings and huge storage capacity.

The manicured grounds offers a range of mature established fruit trees, enclosed chook pen, vegetable gardens, 10000L concrete water tank and massive 9m x 7.5m x 4.6m high clearance powered shed with 3.4m high roller doors front and rear and backing onto seasonal creek reserve no rear neighbours!

This property is offered for sale for the first time in its history and features glorious picturesque mountain views with the aspect to the crystal clear Isabella Falls backdrop.

With so much on offer and potential to expand this property offers space and is set in a premium pocket. This impressive acreage lifestyle represents a potentially lucrative investment in a family friendly scenic location, tucked away in the Edmonton foothills. We welcome your interest.
