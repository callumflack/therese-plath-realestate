Residential property at LITTLE MULGRAVE on a 8.84m2

Offers Over $850,000

LITTLE MULGRAVE 4 2 2

Land area: 8.84m2

Property ID: CSS10075752

Therese Plath has pleasure in presenting this quality hidden gem to prospective buyers who particularly enjoy their own space and tranquillity in the foothills at Little Mulgrave just outside of Gordonvale.

Located 40 minutes from Cairns City on the Southside and only 10 minutes to all conveniences this seller is regrettably relocating and sadly leaving behind lots of happy times and memories.

This country retreat offers all the emphasis on lifestyle of usable land and bordering the most glorious fully shaded swimming holes in the Mulgrave River. The children will love paddling down the river and catching fish for dinner.

Originally constructed by David McCoy Homes this spacious four bedroom home has been freshly painted inside and out. The massive gourmet kitchen serves the family/dining area which opens into the formal lounge and through to the massive delightful timber deck at the rear so ideal for entertaining.

The generous master bedroom is carpeted with en-suite and security screened as with the other bedrooms and the home is fully air conditioned. There is double remote garage and plenty of storage.

The satellite dish ensures all efficiency with faster internet.

A gardener's delight with the front gazebo in the delightful fish pond just perfect for cocktail hour enjoying the scenic setting and the relaxing mountain views.

The large workshop caters for ultra lights and the air strip on the property allows you to fly in and out. There is another 3 bay farm shed with roller doors.

The bonus is the solar 12 panels giving 4KW and there is a bore supplying two large tanks and irrigation.

There is an extensive variety of established tropical fruit trees, some including rare black sapote, lychees and avocados.

Including all farm implements, enclosed chook pen and the option of 11 heads of cattle could be included.

If you are searching for peaceful surrounds then this unique property quietly tucked away is waiting for you. Don't just imagine this fabulous lifestyle live it now!
